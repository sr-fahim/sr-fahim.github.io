  About me

Shahriar Rahman Fahim Received his B. Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering degree from Rajshahi University of Engineering Technology. and M. Sc. degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from American International University- Bangladesh, and his From 2018, he has published 16 journal articles and 24 conference articles with reputed publishers including IEEE, Elsevier, Springer, MDPI, and so on. He has been a reviewer for several journals and served as a committee member for several international conferences. His specialties in power transmission systems are fault detection, fault classification, fault localization, power-quality event analysis, computer relaying, renewable generation, and smart grid systems. His areas of interest are diverse and cover Human-Machine Interaction (HMI) and artificial intelligence. Currently, his research focuses on data-driven predictive maintenance, detection, diagnostics, prognostics, and the application of machine learning techniques in signal processing.
