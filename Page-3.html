Media Appearance

We, "Team reverse bias" participated in a reality show, "Esho Robot Banai" (lets make robot) which was streamed on countries leading TV channel "Channel I" on "Bangla" language.

GPH Ispat Esho Robot Banai, Episode 13

After being selected in the audition round, team Reverse Bias has first present their idea in front of judge. The initial idea was to make a smart driver safety system. The main feature of the proposed idea is a vehicle will detect the drivers drowsiness and take necessary steps. Also, for heavy loaded vehicle the proposed system can measure the load and prevent the overloading conditions. 

GPH Ispat Esho Robot Banai, Episode 19

In this episode, we have built a prototype based on our idea presented in "episode 13". The judges were reviewing the project and asked for clarification.

GPH Ispat Esho Robot Banai, Episode 26

Unlike "Episode 13" here we have to choose from the project idea given by the audience. And we had selected the idea of making a polar coordinated-based writing robotic arm.

Team Reverse Bias

My vision

Since my childhood, I have always liked to see things work and expressed a propensity for exploration. I consistently found something to explore in my father's backyard garage, thoughtful about the science fiction stories, or the lego sets on my shelf. Much of these interests have faded over the years while the intellectual inquisitiveness that they fostered has only strengthened. I have completed my graduate studies with a major focus on power systems and have not yet finished my journey. An objective analysis of my background, interests, strengths, as well as intellectual curiosity, lead me to take advanced knowledge over power engineering. With this degree, I aspire to be a successful researcher in an academic as well as industrial environment.
